Welcome the Viva Voce Oral Exit Exam Platform!

A viva voce, Latin for "with living voice," is a style of examination in which the examinee must orally defend their work. This form of assessment is commonly used in higher education, particularly for advanced degrees such as a Master's thesis or a Doctoral dissertation. In a viva voce, examiners engage directly with candidates, asking questions to evaluate their understanding and mastery of the subject matter. The viva voce is a critical part of many academic journeys, allowing students to demonstrate their knowledge, articulate their research findings, and engage in scholarly discourse. This platform facilitates the preparation and management of viva voce exams, streamlining the process for both examiners and examinees.
